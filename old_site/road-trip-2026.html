Leg 1 Amesbury → Austin
Day 1
Oct 28
Amesbury, MA → Staunton, VA
~550 mi · ~8.5 hrs · Stop: Harpers Ferry
🏠 We leave Amesbury
Early start south on I-95. The big trip begins.
Stop
Harpers Ferry National Historical Park, WV
Where the Shenandoah meets the Potomac - three states visible from one overlook. ~20 min off I-81. Open 9am–5pm.
Sight · Staunton, VA
Blackfriars Playhouse
The world's only re-creation of Shakespeare's original indoor theatre. Even if no show is on, worth a look. 10 S Market St.
Sight
Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library & Museum
Wilson's birthplace in the heart of downtown. Open daily 9am–5pm · $14 adult.
Sight
Frontier Culture Museum
Outdoor living history museum with authentic farmsteads transplanted from Europe and early America. US-250 W · open until 5pm.
Overnight
Staunton, VA
Day 2
Oct 29
Staunton, VA → Paducah, KY
~470 mi · ~7 hrs · Stop: National Quilt Museum
Depart Staunton
West on I-64 toward Lexington, KY.
Stop
National Quilt Museum - Paducah, KY
Paducah is a UNESCO Creative City. The Quilt Museum holds one of the most extraordinary textile collections in the world. Mon–Sat 10am–5pm · $12 adult.
Sight · Paducah, KY
Paducah Floodwall Murals
50+ large-scale murals painted on the floodwall depicting Paducah's history. Free to walk · stretches along Water St downtown.
Sight
Lower Town Arts District
Paducah's artist relocation neighborhood — colorful homes, galleries, and studios. A UNESCO Creative City in full view. Easy walk from downtown.
Sight
Paducah Riverfront & City Marina
Where the Tennessee meets the Ohio. Great sunset spot and a good leg stretch before dinner.
Overnight
Paducah, KY
Day 3
Oct 30
Paducah, KY → Austin, TX via Memphis
~650 mi · ~10 hrs · Big day - early start
Early departure from Paducah
South on US-45 toward Memphis. On the road by 7:30am.
Lunch
Tonica - Memphis, TN
Mediterranean small plates and tapas downtown - graze without getting too full for more driving. 265 S Front St. Budget 1.5 hrs.
Sight · Memphis, TN
Sun Studio
Where Elvis, Johnny Cash, Jerry Lee Lewis, and B.B. King all recorded. 45-min tour runs every hour · 706 Union Ave · $15.
Sight
National Civil Rights Museum
Built around the Lorraine Motel where Dr. King was assassinated. One of the most important museums in America. Open daily · $20 adult.
Sight
Beale Street
The home of the blues. Even a quick walk down the strip before hitting the road is worth it. Live music pours out of every door.
We arrive 🎃
Austin, TX - Halloween Night
Pulling in on Halloween. 6th Street goes all out. Airbnb check-in, then straight into it.
🤠
7 nights · Oct 30 – Nov 6

Austin, Texas

Airbnb stay - cooking, exploring, staying put for a bit. South Congress, Barton Springs, live music. This is the rest stop the whole trip is built around.

Leg 2 Austin → Menomonie
Day 4
Nov 6
Austin, TX → Kansas City, MO
~8.5 hrs · Long haul north - early start
We leave Austin
I-35 NE through Dallas, then north through Oklahoma toward Kansas City.
Museum
National WWI Museum & Memorial - Kansas City
One of the best museums in the country. Stunning architecture and views from the Liberty Memorial tower. Open 10am–5pm. Budget 2–3 hrs if we arrive with daylight.
Sight · Kansas City, MO
Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art
Free admission · world-class collection including one of the best Asian art departments in the country. Open until 9pm Fri–Sat · 4525 Oak St.
Sight
Crossroads Arts District
KC's creative hub — galleries, murals, independent restaurants, and coffee shops. Great evening wander between the museum and dinner.
Sight
Country Club Plaza
America's first outdoor shopping district (1922) — Spanish-inspired architecture, fountains, and good restaurants. 47th & J.C. Nichols Pkwy.
Overnight
Kansas City, MO
Day 5
Nov 7
Kansas City, MO → Lancaster, WI
~490 mi · ~7 hrs · Dinner in Platteville
North from Kansas City
I-35 N into Iowa, then east across into Wisconsin.
Dinner
Steve's Pizza Palace - Platteville, WI
A Platteville institution for over 50 years. Crispy crust, homemade sausage, legendary onion rings. Three full bars. 175 W Main St · Open until 10pm · #2 restaurant in Platteville.
Sight · Platteville, WI
The M — World's Largest Letter
A 214-foot "M" on the hillside above town, lit up at night. Short hike to the top for a panoramic view of the Driftless Area. Free.
Sight
Mining & Rollo Jamison Museums
Platteville's lead and zinc mining history in a fascinating double museum. Real underground mine tour available. 405 E Main St · $10 adult.
Overnight
Lancaster, WI
Day 6
Nov 8
Lancaster, WI → Menomonie, WI 🏁
~3.5 hrs · Easy morning · Almost there
Depart Lancaster after breakfast
US-61 N → WI-35 N → I-94 W. Coffee from the hotel, easy drive.
We arrive 🏁
Menomonie, WI - The Destination
Pulling in around 1:30pm. This is what the trip is for.
Sight · Menomonie, WI
Mabel Tainter Center for the Arts
A stunning 1890 Romanesque theatre built as a memorial — stained glass, hand-carved wood, and a pipe organ. 205 Main St E · free to visit the lobby.
Sight
Lake Menomin & Wakanda Park
A beautiful lake right in town. Wakanda Park has trails, a beach, and views across the water — great morning or evening walk with Batty.
Sight
Wilson Place Museum
The preserved 1846 mansion of lumber baron James Wilson — 40 rooms with original furnishings. One of the best-kept historic homes in Wisconsin.
Leg 3 Menomonie → Home
Day 7
Nov 10
Menomonie, WI → Racine, WI
~3.5 hrs · Easy · Optional Milwaukee lunch
Southeast on I-94
Easy drive toward Milwaukee then south along Lake Michigan to Racine.
Optional
Milwaukee, WI
30 min north of Racine - great lunch stop. Lakefront, Milwaukee Art Museum, strong food scene.
Sight · Racine, WI
SC Johnson Campus — Frank Lloyd Wright
Two Frank Lloyd Wright masterpieces on one campus: the 1939 Administration Building and the Research Tower. Free guided tours available · 1525 Howe St.
Sight
Wind Point Lighthouse
One of the oldest and tallest lighthouses on the Great Lakes, sitting right on Lake Michigan. Peaceful walk, beautiful views. Free · open grounds.
Sight
Racine Art Museum (RAM)
One of the top contemporary craft collections in North America. Strong fiber arts holdings — fitting after Paducah. 441 Main St · $5 suggested.
Overnight
Racine, WI
Day 8
Nov 11
Racine, WI → Buffalo, NY
~7 hrs · Optional Presque Isle stop
East on I-94, then I-90
Through Chicago suburbs, into Indiana and Ohio along the Lake Erie corridor.
Optional Stop
Presque Isle State Park - Erie, PA
Right off I-90, no detour. A sandy peninsula into Lake Erie with beaches and a lighthouse. Beautiful in November. Good leg stretch.
Sight · Buffalo, NY
Buffalo AKG Art Museum
Just reopened after a $230M expansion — one of the oldest and best modern art collections in the US. Picasso, Warhol, Frida Kahlo. 1285 Elmwood Ave.
Sight
Darwin Martin House — Frank Lloyd Wright
Considered one of Wright's greatest Prairie Style achievements (1905). Guided tours daily · 125 Jewett Pkwy · $20 adult. Book ahead.
Sight
Elmwood Village
Buffalo's most walkable neighborhood — independent shops, cafes, and restaurants along Elmwood Ave. Great dinner and evening stroll spot.
Overnight
Buffalo, NY

Optional
Niagara Falls State Park, NY
