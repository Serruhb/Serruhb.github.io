What we do

Four ways in, depending on what's actually broken

Every engagement ends the same way, with your team able to run the system without us. How we get there depends on where things stand today.

01
Systems Recovery & Diagnosis

We find out how things actually work before we touch anything. Two to three sessions of honest assessment before any prescription.

  • Operational system audits
  • Fragmented data diagnosis
  • Stakeholder + SME interviews
  • Gap analysis between leadership perception and operational reality
  • Phased roadmap with realistic ROI

02
Automation & Integration Stabilization

We stabilize what is broken and rebuild what cannot scale. No automation applied to a broken process and called solved.

  • Workflow redesign and data model reconstruction
  • Salesforce, Workato, Jira, Snowflake integration
  • AI agent design with governance and auditability
  • Subscriptions, intake, and triage automation
  • Edge case handling and human review layers

03
AI Implementation with Governance

Practical, responsible AI. We build intelligent systems that are deterministic where they need to be and agentic where they should be.

  • AI readiness assessment
  • Agentic workflow architecture
  • Confidence scoring and structured outputs
  • Audit trails and output controls
  • Documentation embedded in delivery, not bolted on after

04
Knowledge Transfer & Operating Model Design

We build the understanding around the system, not just the system. Every engagement ends with your team able to run and evolve what we built.

  • Handoff documentation and knowledge base alignment
  • Multi-stakeholder clarity sessions
  • Leadership dashboards and KPI frameworks
  • Training embedded throughout, not at the end
  • We step back when the right person is trained or hired
