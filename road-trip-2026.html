Leg 1 Amesbury → Austin
Day 1
Oct 28
Amesbury → Staunton, VA
~550 miles · ~8.5 hrs · Stop: Harpers Ferry
▾
🏠 We leave Amesbury
Early start south on I-95. The big trip begins.
Stop
Harpers Ferry National Historical Park, WV
Where the Shenandoah meets the Potomac — three states visible from one overlook. About 20 minutes off I-81. Open 9am–5pm.
Overnight
The Blackburn Inn — Staunton, VA
A former 1828 asylum turned Michelin-listed boutique hotel on 80 acres of rolling Virginia hills. Spa, on-site restaurant, and a genuinely memorable stay. ★★★★½ · Service animal welcome.
Day 2
Oct 29
Staunton → Paducah, KY
~470 miles · ~7 hrs · Stop: National Quilt Museum
▾
Depart Blackburn Inn
West on I-64 toward Lexington, KY.
Stop
National Quilt Museum — Paducah, KY
Paducah is a UNESCO Creative City. The Quilt Museum is extraordinary — one of the largest collections of contemporary quilts in the world. Open Mon–Sat 10am–5pm. $12 adult. Budget 1.5 hrs.
Overnight
The 1857 Hotel — Paducah, KY
Boutique rooms with rainfall showers and soaking tubs in the heart of Paducah's historic downtown. 9.6/10 Expedia · Service animal welcome — no fee.

Leg 2 Austin → Menomonie
Day 4
Nov 6
Austin → Kansas City, MO
~8.5 hrs · Long haul north — early start
▾
We leave Austin
I-35 northeast through Dallas, then north through Oklahoma. Long day — coffee first.
Museum
National WWI Museum & Memorial — Kansas City
One of the best museums in the country — stunning architecture, world-class exhibits, panoramic views from the Liberty Memorial tower. Open 10am–5pm. Budget 2–3 hrs if we arrive with daylight.
Overnight
Kansas City, MO — Hotel TBD
Choosing between Cherry Warehouse Hotel (boutique 1930 warehouse, Midwest Living best new hotel 2026) and Fairfield Inn KC Downtown (~$120, free breakfast). Deciding closer to the date.

Nov 7
Kansas City → Lancaster, WI
~490 miles · ~7 hrs · Dinner in Platteville
▾
North from Kansas City
I-35 N into Iowa, then east across into Wisconsin.
Dinner
Steve's Pizza Palace — Platteville, WI
A Platteville institution for over 50 years. Crispy crust, homemade sausage, and onion rings that people drive hours for. Three full bars. 175 W Main St. Open until 10pm. #2 restaurant in Platteville.
Overnight
Sleep Inn & Suites Lancaster-Platteville — Lancaster, WI
14 miles from Platteville — just somewhere clean to sleep before the final push to Menomonie. Free hot breakfast. 4.4★ · ~$116/night.

Leg 3 Menomonie → Home
Day 7
Nov 10
Menomonie → Racine, WI
~3.5 hrs · Short day · Optional Milwaukee stop
▾
Southeast on I-94
Easy drive toward Milwaukee and then south to Racine along Lake Michigan.
Optional
Milwaukee, WI
30 minutes north of Racine — great for a lunch stop. Lakefront, Milwaukee Art Museum, strong food scene.
Overnight
Hotel Verdant — Racine, WI
LEED Gold certified boutique hotel right in downtown Racine, 5 minutes from Lake Michigan. Dinner at Marguerite (hearth-fired Italian), drinks at rooftop bar Eave with panoramic lake views. #1 hotel in Racine · 9.2/10. Service animal welcome.
Day 8
Nov 11
Racine → Buffalo, NY
~7 hrs · Optional Presque Isle stop in Erie
▾
East on I-94, then I-90
Through Chicago suburbs, into Indiana and Ohio along the Lake Erie corridor.
Optional Stop
Presque Isle State Park — Erie, PA
Right on I-90, no detour needed. A sandy peninsula jutting into Lake Erie with beaches and a lighthouse. Striking in November. Good leg stretch.
Overnight
Hampton Inn & Suites Buffalo Downtown — Buffalo, NY
Housed in the historic 1920 Jackson Building in downtown Buffalo. Free breakfast, indoor pool and hot tub, walkable to restaurants. ★★★★½ · 1,527 reviews. Service animal welcome, no fee. Niagara Falls is 25 minutes away — worth a morning detour on Nov 12.
