The Full Route

Every driving day, every stop, every overnight - in order.

Leg 1 Amesbury → Austin
Day 1
Oct 28
Amesbury, MA → Staunton, VA
~550 mi · ~8.5 hrs · Stop: Harpers Ferry
🏠 We leave Amesbury
Early start south on I-95. The big trip begins.
Stop
Harpers Ferry National Historical Park, WV
Where the Shenandoah meets the Potomac - three states visible from one overlook. ~20 min off I-81. Open 9am–5pm.
Overnight
The Blackburn Inn - Staunton, VA
A former 1828 asylum turned Michelin-listed boutique hotel on 80 acres of rolling Virginia hills. Spa, on-site restaurant. ★★★★½ · Service animal welcome.
Day 2
Oct 29
Staunton, VA → Paducah, KY
~470 mi · ~7 hrs · Stop: National Quilt Museum
Depart Blackburn Inn
West on I-64 toward Lexington, KY.
Stop
National Quilt Museum - Paducah, KY
Paducah is a UNESCO Creative City. The Quilt Museum holds one of the most extraordinary textile collections in the world. Mon–Sat 10am–5pm · $12 adult.
Overnight
The 1857 Hotel - Paducah, KY
Boutique rooms with rainfall showers and soaking tubs in historic downtown Paducah. 9.6/10 Expedia · Service animal welcome, no fee.
Day 3
Oct 30
Paducah, KY → Austin, TX via Memphis
~650 mi · ~10 hrs · Big day - early start
Early departure from Paducah
South on US-45 toward Memphis. On the road by 7:30am.
Lunch
Tonica - Memphis, TN
Mediterranean small plates and tapas downtown - graze without getting too full for more driving. 265 S Front St. Budget 1.5 hrs.
We arrive 🎃
Austin, TX - Halloween Night
Pulling in on Halloween. 6th Street goes all out. Airbnb check-in, then straight into it.
🤠
7 nights · Oct 30 – Nov 6

Austin, Texas

Airbnb stay - cooking, exploring, staying put for a bit. South Congress, Barton Springs, live music. This is the rest stop the whole trip is built around.

Leg 2 Austin → Menomonie
Day 4
Nov 6
Austin, TX → Kansas City, MO
~8.5 hrs · Long haul north - early start
We leave Austin
I-35 NE through Dallas, then north through Oklahoma toward Kansas City.
Museum
National WWI Museum & Memorial - Kansas City
One of the best museums in the country. Stunning architecture and views from the Liberty Memorial tower. Open 10am–5pm. Budget 2–3 hrs if we arrive with daylight.
Overnight
Kansas City, MO - Hotel TBD
Deciding between Cherry Warehouse Hotel (boutique 1930 warehouse, Midwest Living best new hotel 2026) and Fairfield Inn KC Downtown (~$120, free breakfast). Confirming closer to the date.
Day 5
Nov 7
Kansas City, MO → Lancaster, WI
~490 mi · ~7 hrs · Dinner in Platteville
North from Kansas City
I-35 N into Iowa, then east across into Wisconsin.
Dinner
Steve's Pizza Palace - Platteville, WI
A Platteville institution for over 50 years. Crispy crust, homemade sausage, legendary onion rings. Three full bars. 175 W Main St · Open until 10pm · #2 restaurant in Platteville.
Overnight
Sleep Inn & Suites Lancaster-Platteville - Lancaster, WI
14 miles from Platteville. Clean, easy overnight before the final push to Menomonie. Free hot breakfast. 4.4★ · ~$116/night.
Day 6
Nov 8
Lancaster, WI → Menomonie, WI 🏁
~3.5 hrs · Easy morning · Almost there
Depart Lancaster after breakfast
US-61 N → WI-35 N → I-94 W. Coffee from the hotel, easy drive.
We arrive 🏁
Menomonie, WI - The Destination
Pulling in around 1:30pm. Hampton Inn & Suites UW Stout - top-rated in town, free breakfast. This is what the trip is for.
Leg 3 Menomonie → Home
Day 7
Nov 10
Menomonie, WI → Racine, WI
~3.5 hrs · Easy · Optional Milwaukee lunch
Southeast on I-94
Easy drive toward Milwaukee then south along Lake Michigan to Racine.
Optional
Milwaukee, WI
30 min north of Racine - great lunch stop. Lakefront, Milwaukee Art Museum, strong food scene.
Overnight
Hotel Verdant - Racine, WI
LEED Gold certified boutique hotel downtown, 5 min from Lake Michigan. Dinner at Marguerite (hearth-fired Italian), drinks at rooftop bar Eave with panoramic lake views. #1 hotel in Racine · 9.2/10. Service animal welcome.
Day 8
Nov 11
Racine, WI → Buffalo, NY
~7 hrs · Optional Presque Isle stop
East on I-94, then I-90
Through Chicago suburbs, into Indiana and Ohio along the Lake Erie corridor.
Optional Stop
Presque Isle State Park - Erie, PA
Right off I-90, no detour. A sandy peninsula into Lake Erie with beaches and a lighthouse. Beautiful in November. Good leg stretch.
Overnight
Hampton Inn & Suites Buffalo Downtown - Buffalo, NY
Historic 1920 Jackson Building in downtown Buffalo. Free breakfast, indoor pool & hot tub, walkable to restaurants. ★★★★½ · 1,527 reviews. Service animal welcome, no fee. Niagara Falls is 25 min away - worth a morning detour.
Day 9
Nov 12
Buffalo, NY → Amesbury, MA 🏠
~6 hrs · Final day · Optional Niagara detour
Depart Buffalo
Optional: 25 min to Niagara Falls State Park first - free entry, dramatically misty in November.
Optional
Niagara Falls State Park, NY
Free entry · Horseshoe Falls from Prospect Point · Less crowded in November · Budget 1.5 hrs.
Home 🏠
Amesbury, MA - We made it.
I-90 E → Albany → I-90 E → I-495 → I-95 N. ~4,400 miles. 16 days. Batty approved. Done.
