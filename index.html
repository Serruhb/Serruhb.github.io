Intro

How I think about systems, automation, and impact.

I design systems that turn scattered data into clear, reliable decisions. My work sits at the intersection of enterprise platforms, automation, and human workflows; where small errors can quietly compound and good systems make teams faster, calmer, and more effective.

My background in Mathematics and Chemistry shaped how I approach engineering: I look for structure and failure modes before I reach for tools. Programming became an expressive way to model complex systems and make them observable, testable, and safe at scale.

Before enterprise automation, I owned a dog training business and worked as a math and chemistry tutor for high school and college students; using automation to track outcomes, manage operations, and reduce manual effort. That grounded my instincts in real constraints: unclear ownership, noisy data, and systems that must work for humans.

Today I build AI-assisted and rule-driven systems with guardrails, auditability, and human-in-the-loop control—especially in high-stakes areas like healthcare, revenue, customer experience, and operational decision-making.

OTA Monitoring (Performance Alert & Monitoring)

Explainable alerting + monitoring framework that converts weekly OTA metrics into prioritized, owner-aware tickets and watchlist signals.
