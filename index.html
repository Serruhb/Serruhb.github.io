An Introduction

I was born in Franklin, Louisiana on February 11, 1986. While completing primary education I moved with family and ended up attending schools in New Hampshire, South Carolina and Florida. Growing up and moving around gave me an advantage on learning how to network and meet people. I am incredibly social and rarely meet a stranger.

My passion for numbers and data began while attending college at Columbia College in South Carolina I studied Mathematics and Chemistry. Going to a smaller college allowed professors to notice my natural inclination towards computer science. I recently graduated from the University of Texas for Data Analytics, this program has only made my fondness for math and data grow. I am excited for the future and ready to be your next asset!
