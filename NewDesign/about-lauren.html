Lauren's career has unfolded almost entirely at the point where operational execution and health system partnership meet. Her platform fluency runs deep across Epic, QGenda, Power BI, SOC2 compliance architecture, and ServiceNow, but the technology is always in service of a decision someone needs to make. The throughline is consistent: build the infrastructure that makes performance visible, then hold it accountable.
